Brief Introduction.

I am a Mechanical Engineer by education and a Software Engineer by practice, constantly driven by a cycle of learning, teaching, and growth. With an interdisciplinary background ranging from Biomechanics to intelligent systems, I am now heading to graduate school to continue my research into making surgical and robotic systems more intelligent, adaptive, precise, and human-centered.

I am an energetic and purposeful learner. Currently, I work as a Software Engineer at 4Sale International, one of the largest marketplaces in the MENA region. Concurrently, I serve as a TA/RA at both Fayoum University and Fayoum National University. In 2023, I completed a post-graduate diploma in Computer Sciences from the Information Technology Institute (ITI), one of the most selective institutes in Egypt (6% selectivity rate), graduating with the highest honors. Moreover, I earned my Bachelor’s degree in Engineering in 2020, achieving the highest honors in each of the five years of my studies, averaging a nearly CGPA of 4 (CGPA: 3.95).

My research interests converge at the intersection of mechanical engineering, AI, and biomedical applications, anchored by three foundational pillars: excellence, impact, and reward. I am committed to harnessing AI and robotics to develop intelligent biomedical systems that elevate surgical precision and improve patient outcomes. This endeavor is profoundly rewarding as a lifelong learner dedicated to achieving excellence in advancing healthcare through innovative robotic solutions.
