## üìà **Key Metrics Explained**

### Engagement Metrics

- **Average engagement time**: 
  - Time users actively interact with your site
  - Includes scrolling, clicking, video watching
  - Excludes idle time

- **Engaged sessions**:
  - Sessions lasting 10+ seconds
  - Or with 2+ page views
  - Or with 1+ conversion event

- **Bounce rate**:
  - Percentage of single-page sessions
  - Lower is better (indicates engagement)

### Device Metrics

- **Sessions by device**: Total visits per device type
- **Users by device**: Unique visitors per device
- **Conversion rate by device**: Goal completions per device

## üìä **Understanding Your Data**

### What Good Metrics Look Like:

- **Average engagement time**: 
  - Portfolio sites: 1-3 minutes is good
  - Blog posts: 2-5 minutes
  - Landing pages: 30 seconds - 2 minutes

- **Bounce rate**:
  - Portfolio sites: 40-60% is normal
  - Below 40% is excellent
  - Above 70% needs attention

- **Device distribution**:
  - Modern sites: 50-70% mobile
  - Professional portfolios: 40-60% desktop

## üõ†Ô∏è **Troubleshooting**

### If you don't see data:

1. **Wait 24-48 hours**: GA4 can take time to populate
2. **Check Real-Time**: Verify tracking is working
3. **Verify Measurement ID**: Ensure `G-V0BVDVF0Q5` is correct
4. **Check browser console**: Look for GA4 errors (F12)

### If data seems incorrect:

1. **Check date range**: Default is last 7 days
2. **Verify filters**: Make sure no filters are excluding data
3. **Check sampling**: Large date ranges may be sampled
